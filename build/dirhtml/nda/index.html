19. Historical Non-Disclosure Agreement¶

The following NDA comes from the time when RhostMUSH was not publially available. It is preserved here for historical reasons. We are suckers for looking back at things. :) –Ambrosia

  1. I agree, to not give out the code, in part or in full, in any form of medium, to anyone or anything not previously allowed by the developers.

  2. I agree, to not let others look at the code, in part or in full, in any form of medium, to anyone or anything not previously allowed by the developers.

  3. I am aware that any modifications I make to the code is _FULLY_ permitted, and that I do _NOT_ have to return said patches to the developers.

Note

The Rhost developers would like to see what was added, and possibly look at adding them to the main distro if we see others would like it (with full credits to you), but we belive once you have the code, you should be allowed to play with it fully as long as the first two rules are kept.
